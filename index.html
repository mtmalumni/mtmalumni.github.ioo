Who We Are

Bridging the Past,
Present & Future

The MTM Alumni Association stands as a testament to the enduring bonds forged at MTM Wafy College, Chokli—a prominent institution under the Coordination of Islamic Colleges (CIC). Guided by the noble vision of our college management, the association is dedicated to fostering a lifelong relationship between the institution and its Wafy graduates.

Our mission is to build a vibrant, global community of alumni through the MTM Alumni Portal, ensuring that members support one another and contribute to the continued excellence of our alma mater. Through collaborative initiatives, knowledge sharing, and mentorship hubs, we strive to empower every alumnus while keeping the spirit of brotherhood alive.

Join us in preserving our shared heritage and shaping a brighter future.
